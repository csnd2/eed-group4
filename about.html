Our Story...

The Entrepreneurship Development Center at Federal Polytechnic Nekede was established to address the growing need for practical entrepreneurial skills among group 2 students in EED Practical. As an institution committed to producing job creators rather than job seekers, the Federal Polytechnic Nekede initiated this center to equip students with hands-on skills and knowledge necessary for successful business ventures.

Since our inception, we have trained thousands of students across various disciplines, helping them develop marketable skills, create business plans, and launch successful startups. Our comprehensive approach combines theoretical knowledge with practical experience, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared to face the challenges of entrepreneurship in Nigeria's dynamic economy.

Today, the EDC stands as a beacon of innovation and entrepreneurial excellence, continuously evolving our programs to meet the changing demands of the business landscape and empowering the next generation of Nigerian entrepreneurs.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Guided by strong principles and a clear direction, we work to create impactful change through entrepreneurship education.

Our Mission

To empower students with entrepreneurial skills, knowledge, and mindset that enable them to create sustainable businesses, generate employment, and contribute to economic growth.

Our Vision

To be the leading entrepreneurship development center in Nigeria, recognized for excellence in entrepreneurial education and the success of our student entrepreneurs in the global marketplace.

Our Values

• Excellence in teaching and learning
• Integrity and ethical conduct
• Practical, hands-on approach to education
• Collaboration and partnership
