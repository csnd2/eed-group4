About Us

Learn about the Entrepreneurship Development Center at Federal Polytechnic Nekede and our mission to empower students with entrepreneurial skills.

Know More.

The Entrepreneurship Development Center (EDC) at Federal Polytechnic Nekede was created to meet the increasing demand for practical entrepreneurial skills among Group 4 students in EED Practical. As an institution dedicated to fostering job creators instead of job seekers, the Federal Polytechnic Nekede established this center to provide students with the hands-on skills and knowledge essential for successful business ventures.

Since our founding, we have trained thousands of students from diverse fields, assisting them in developing marketable skills, crafting business plans, and launching successful startups. Our holistic approach merges theoretical knowledge with practical experience, ensuring that our graduates are well-equipped to tackle the challenges of entrepreneurship in Nigeria's evolving economy.

Today, the EDC serves as a symbol of innovation and entrepreneurial excellence, continually adapting our programs to align with the shifting demands of the business environment and empowering the next generation of Nigerian entrepreneurs.

Our Goals, Vision, & Core Beliefs

Driven by core principles and a distinct purpose, we strive to make a meaningful difference through entrepreneurial education.

Our Goal

To empower students with entrepreneurial skills, knowledge, and mindset that enable them to create sustainable businesses, generate employment, and contribute to economic growth.

Core Beliefs

  • Excellence in teaching and learning
  • Integrity and ethical conduct
  • Practical, hands-on approach to education
  • Collaboration and partnership

Our Vision

To be the leading entrepreneurship development center in Nigeria, recognized for excellence in entrepreneurial education and the success of our student entrepreneurs in the global marketplace.
