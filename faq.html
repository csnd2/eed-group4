FAQ

Discover answers to frequently asked questions regarding the Entrepreneurship Development Center and our offerings.

What is the Entrepreneurship Development Center ?

The Entrepreneurship Development Center at Federal Polytechnic Nekede is a specialized facility dedicated to equipping students with entrepreneurial skills, practical training, and resources needed to start and manage successful businesses. We offer training in various skills, mentorship, and support for student entrepreneurs.

Do you offer internship opportunities ?

The EDC facilitates internship placements with partner businesses and organizations for our students. These internships provide valuable real-world experience and professional connections relevant to students' entrepreneurial interests.

How do you provide support for students setups ?

Yes, we offer incubation support for promising student startups, including mentorship, workspace, networking opportunities, and potential funding connections. Students with viable business ideas can apply for our incubation program through a competitive selection process.

Are there networking events for enterprenuer ?

Yes, the EDC regularly organizes networking events, business fairs, pitch competitions, and guest speaker sessions that connect students with industry professionals, potential investors, and fellow entrepreneurs.

What skill can I learn at the EDC ?

The EDC offers training in a wide range of skills including digital marketing, web development, graphic design, photography, business management, financial literacy, market research, and more. Our programs are regularly updated to reflect current industry demands and entrepreneurial trends.
